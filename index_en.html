Urgent information

The executive comittee of Maschinendeck Trier e.V., following the orders of the german Department of the Interior , has made the decision, to shut down the hackespace, beginning on

18th March 2020

until further notice. No events will take place. We ask you cordially for your understanding, and send the best wishes, especially regarding health, to your and your family.

The Club

With the Maschinendeck e.V., we have set ourselves the goal of creating a place in Trier where all kinds of nerd cultures can find a home. Whether you're interested in coding, soldering, coffee roasting or want to play board games: You are always welcome here.
